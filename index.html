About

In the spring of 2015, two dudes from Massachusetts got sick of their boring lives and decided to do something about it. Founding fathers Dylan Ferreira and Eric Mead plugged their microphones into their fitting XLR cables, strapped on some guitars, and began making some very loud but satisfying noises; noises we now know as Snow Day.

Snow Day was smooth sailing through the local music scene, producing their first album "Two Hour Delay" (Eldersound Studios and Lurssen Mastering). Two Hour Delay set the vibe for Snow Day with party rock melody and persistent harmony.

Snow Day received great recognition by topping local music radio: Boston Rock Radio, making #1 for most requested song with the albums hit, "A Pretty kind of Nothing" Snow Day also attracted sponsorships from brands such as Fireball Whiskey and Worcester PRIDE.

Former and beloved drummer Christian Scavo had to leave the band to pursue an amazing career opportunity and travel the world (we could not deny him of such a great time and experience). This led to the entry of Jake Killoy into the band who brings a flashy yet laid back drumming style, quick jazzy fills, and a wave of happiness and positivity.

Snow Day continues to make original, mildly offensive yet catchy music. Some wonderful things are to come and we hope you all are ready to join us in this journey.
